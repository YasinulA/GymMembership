Overview

Our gym has two floors and a basement. The second floor is the cardio room, and the first floor is where all the weight lifting equipment are located. Our basement is where we have the boxing training.

Cardio Room

Welcome to the limitless gym's cardio room. It is located in the second floor of the gym. It is our most luxurious room and here, you off your calories to your heart's content to get to your goals.

Weight Room

Welcome to the limitless gym's weight room. It is located in the first floor of the gym. Also, here you may either work by yourself or with a trainer to get to your goals. Please note that in order to have a trainer you must have the premium gym membership.

Boxing Room

Welcome to the limitless gym's boxing room. It is located in the basement of the gym. Here, you can train to be a boxer under the watch of our professional trainers. You must have the premium gym membership in order to be use this facility.
